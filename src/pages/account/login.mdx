## First Time

Click on **Login** button in the **Welcome Screen**. Alternatively, you may type the address for login through [Login Page](https://da-ipms2020.web.app/login).

![Logging in to your account](/images/login.png)

1. In the form, input your **email** and **password**.

1. Click the **login** button.

1. After logging in for the first time, you need to change your password as the IPD is still aware of your password. For instruction, go to [changing your password](/account/changing-password).

1. Verify your email to ensure that you can receive updates from the system and access other security features. For instruction, go to [verifying your email address](/account/verify-email).

1. Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the following screen:

![successful login](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/29625844/87874185-6d3de000-c9fa-11ea-8a30-1c633d343863.png)

Click on **Go to App** to go to the app.

## Succeeding

Follow the above steps from 1 to 3.
