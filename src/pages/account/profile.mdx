## Updating Profile

To update the user profile, click the **Edit Profile** button. You may change the **name**, **nickname**, **position/designation**, and **contact no**.

![update_profile](/images/profile.png)

## Change Avatar

To change the avatar, click the **camera** button on the image placeholder.

### Select Avatar

You may select from previously uploaded images or upload your own image.

![Select avatar](/images/profile_select_avatar.png)

### Upload Avatar

You may upload a new image limited to 50KB.

![Upload avatar](/images/profile_upload_avatar.png)
