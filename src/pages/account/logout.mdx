<PageDescription>

To improve user experience, users are not logged out automatically by the application. User must do this manually.

</PageDescription>

## Logout current device

To logout from the current device, click on **User Image** to reveal the dropdown and click on **Logout**. This will log you out from the device.

![Logout](/images/logout.png)

## Logout all devices

To logout all devices, navigate to the **Security Page**, find **Logout All Devices** and click the **Logout** button.

![Logout all devices](/images/logout_all_devices.png)
